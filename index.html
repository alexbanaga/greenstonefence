Vinyl Fencing

Greenstone offers vinyl fencing to meet your every need. With a variety of fence styles, colors and accessories – you are free to create a secluded space for entertaining and relaxing or add charm and curb appeal to your home. Greenstone’s vinyl fencing is low maintenance, so you can forget about ever needing to paint, sand or stain your fence again! Our 100% vinyl PVC combines the most stringent quality standards with the most innovative compounds to produce a product that will not peel, flake, corrode, rot, rust or attract termites.

Product Features

  • A large variety of styles
  • Available in most popular colors – White, Tan, Khaki
  • Custom Gates for every style
  • Full line of accessories available
  • Made in USA

Wood

Greenstone’s Western Red Cedar is ideal for your home. For centuries Western Red Cedar has been known for its beauty and durability, making it the preferred wood species for outdoor projects. Western Red Cedar fences are extremely sturdy and can last for 20 years.

Product Features

  • Natural Beauty
  • Strength
  • Longevity
  • Stainability

Chain Link Fencing

Greenstone’s Chain Link Fences offer a cost-effective and durable option to securing your yard. This popular selection is affordable, durable and easy to maintain. Available in a galvanized, black, green, or brown finish. Vinyl slats may be added for additional privacy.

Product Features

  • Economical
  • Great for Pet enclosures and perimeter security
  • Full line of accessories available to customize the fence
  • Made in USA

Ornamental Iron Fencing

Greenstone’s Ornamental Iron fences are offered in a high-quality, pre-galvanized steel that is resistant to wear and available in many heights, styles, and colors. Iron provides your property with an elegant, yet durable, fence. Ornamental Iron is most commonly selected in black but is available in other colors as well.

Product Features

  • EWide selection of styles to complement any residential or commercial property application
  • Pre-assembled sections for quick and easy installation
  • Gates are welded rail and picket for added strength and durability
  • Full line of accessories available to customize the fence
  • Made in USA

About Us

GreenStone was established in 2007 on the groundwork of reliability and integrity. A small family business at the time, our primary focus was landscape installation and landscape maintenance. By 2009 our Arbor Division was founded, specializing in Tree maintenance & tree removal. GreenStone Fence has been created on the same principles that have grown our landscape and tree divisions into southern California’s premier outdoor contractor. Since day 1, GreeStone has always fabricated and installed our own fences and gates. Our wide range of skills, allow us to have more control over job timelines and job quality, eliminating the need for sub-contractors. Whether it’s a lifted concrete slab, a huge tree stump or a failing foundation that has damaged your existing fence you can trust that GreenStone Fence has got you covered. From the minute you meet with a Greenstone Fence rep the primary focus is on the SOLUTION with the most practical well thought out approach in my mind. The “GreenStone Difference“ as we like to call it, is what separates us from the competition. Our extensive line up of equipment and knowledgeable staff will ensure that your job is done right the first time and always with SAFETY at the forefront.
